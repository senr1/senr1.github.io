Intro

Hi! I am Rajyashree, a.k.a. Rai (pronounced ‘rye’ like the whiskey) . I am an improviser based in New York City and a neuroscientist at Columbia University . Raised in India and now an immigrant, scientist, and improviser, my aim is to bring my unique lens to a dynamic and collaborative improv team.
