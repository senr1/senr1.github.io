Our Mission

Our mission is to make neuroscience accessible and engaging through improv comedy.




Our Shows

We interview neuroscientists whose science and life inspire our shows. Our shows aim to humanize scientists, demystify neuroscience, and provide practical advice to young people interested in research.

Our History

This project was launched by a Seed Grant from Columbia University's Public Engagement Program in 2024. We now operate from our artistic home at Magnet Theater in New York City.
