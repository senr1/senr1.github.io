Intro

Hi! I am Rajyashree, a.k.a. Rai (pronounced ‘rye’ – as in the whiskey) . I was born and raised in India, but now I am an improviser based in New York City and a neuroscientist at Columbia University . As a scientist and improviser, I aim to bring my unique combination of skills and experiences to a dynamic and collaborative improv team.
